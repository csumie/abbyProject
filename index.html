The Perfect Açai Bowl
Açai Bowls are a typical Brazillian dish made of frozen and mashed açai palm from the Amazonian region. It is
served like a smoothie in a bowl or in a glass. When açai burst into the health food sceen years ago, it was hailed
as a superfruit. Açai is rich in antioxidants, fibers and healthy fats. There are endless possibilities on how to
make the perfect açai bowl for you. Here we will be learning about 4 amazing bowls that will
satisfy every breakfast tummy.
