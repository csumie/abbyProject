RECIPE FOR
The Classic Açai Bowl
The base of an acai bowl is frozen acai berry puree (Sambazon is a great brand of acai berry
puree to use), frozen bananas (to add thicker consistency) and
your choice of dairy (almond milk, coconut milk or cashew milk is great). Notes:
you can purchase acai packs in the frozen fruit section
of many grocery/ health food stores. Look for the frozen pulp, not the powder, to
use in smoothie bowls with acai base.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Total Time: 10 mins
Serving Size: 1

Ingredients:

1 frozen banana
1 pack frozen acai berry puree (Sambazon acai packs are great)
1 cup frozen strawberries
½ cup almond milk (or milk of liquid of choice ie apple juice)
½ to 1 tablespoon honey to taste

Instructions:

1. Combine frozen banana, strawberries, almond milk, honey and acai berry puree to a blender.
Blend until creamy and smooth, adding extra almond milk as needed
to get the blender running. Aim for consistency of a frozen-yogurt

2. Spoon the acai mixture into your favorite bowl or into a nature made bowl like a pineapple
or coconut. Top with your favorite toppings: favorite fresh fruit
sliced: pineapple, strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, kiwi, mango, etc
